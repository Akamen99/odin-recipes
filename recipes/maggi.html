Maggie

Home

Ingredients:

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles (with the tastemaker)
  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter
  • 1/4 cup onions (finely chopped)
  • 1/4 cup carrots (thinly sliced or diced)
  • 1/4 cup bell peppers (finely chopped)
  • 1 green chili (optional, finely chopped)
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic (minced)
  • 1/4 teaspoon red chili powder (optional, for spice)
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon soy sauce (optional, for umami)
  • 1/4 cup water (for cooking)
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Steps:

  1. Boil the Maggi noodles in 1.5 cups of water for 2 minutes until slightly undercooked. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil or butter in a pan. Add garlic and sauté for 30 seconds.
  3. Add onions, green chili, and sauté until onions turn translucent.
  4. Toss in carrots and bell peppers. Cook for 2-3 minutes until slightly tender.
  5. Sprinkle turmeric powder, red chili powder, and the Maggi tastemaker. Mix well.
  6. Add soy sauce (if using) and stir to combine.
  7. Add the boiled Maggi noodles to the pan. Toss everything together for 1-2 minutes.
  8. If the mixture feels dry, add 1-2 tablespoons of water to loosen it up.
  9. Turn off the heat and garnish with fresh coriander leaves.
  10. Serve hot with a squeeze of lemon for extra zing!
