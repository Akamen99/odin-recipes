Chocolate Cake

Home

Description

A perfectly moist and fluffy chocolate cake with a rich chocolate flavor, ideal for any celebration or sweet craving.

Ingredients

For the cake:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 3/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder (high-quality)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs (at room temperature)
  • 1 cup whole milk (at room temperature)
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil (or melted butter)
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1 cup hot water (or hot coffee for enhanced chocolate flavor)

For the chocolate frosting:

  1. 1 cup unsalted butter (softened)
  2. 1 1/4 cups unsweetened cocoa powder
  3. 5 cups powdered sugar (sifted)
  4. 2/3 cup whole milk (or heavy cream)
  5. 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  6. Pinch of salt
