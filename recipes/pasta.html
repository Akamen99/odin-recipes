Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti, penne, or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili flakes (adjust to taste)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
  • 1/4 cup fresh basil (chopped)
  • Salt (to taste)
  • Black pepper (to taste)
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • 1/4 cup pasta water (reserved)

Steps:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to package instructions until al dente. Reserve 1/4 cup of pasta water, then drain the pasta.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  3. Add minced garlic and red chili flakes to the pan. Saut√© for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
  4. Add cherry tomatoes to the pan and cook for 3-4 minutes until they soften and release their juices.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the pan and toss to coat it evenly with the sauce.
  6. If the pasta seems dry, add a little reserved pasta water (1-2 tablespoons at a time) to loosen it up.
  7. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  8. Turn off the heat and stir in fresh basil.
  9. Serve hot, topped with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
